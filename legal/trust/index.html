Legal and Compliance

Trust & Safety

Retensiq is designed for educators. Protecting student data and maintaining responsible use of technology are central to how the platform is built and operated.

Retensiq is operated by Duckpin Technology LLC, a Pennsylvania limited liability company located in West Grove, Pennsylvania, United States.

Our Core Commitments

Student Data Is Never Sold

Retensiq does not sell, rent, or trade student information.

Student data is never used for advertising, marketing targeting, or data brokerage.

Student Work Is Used Only to Provide the Service

Assignments and responses submitted to Retensiq are used solely to generate assessment questions and analytical insights for educators.

Students retain ownership of their submitted work.

Educators Remain in Control

Teachers and institutions control how Retensiq is used within their classrooms.

Educators decide:

  • When assignments are evaluated
  • How results are interpreted
  • Whether and how insights are incorporated into grading or academic workflows

Retensiq Verifies Understanding - It Does Not Accuse Students

Retensiq does not determine whether content was generated by AI.

Instead, the platform helps educators verify whether a student can demonstrate understanding of submitted work.

Results are intended to support educators, not replace professional judgment.

FERPA-Aligned Data Practices

Retensiq is designed to support schools in meeting their obligations under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

When used by educators or institutions, Retensiq operates as a service provider processing information solely for educational purposes.

Secure Platform Infrastructure

We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Access controls and secure infrastructure are used to protect system integrity.
